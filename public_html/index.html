Who Am I ?

About Me

My name is Ashraf Budha Jaku, and I’m a licensed tour guide with over 23 years of experience showing visitors the true beauty of Zanzibar.
I offer personalized and professional tours across the island—from historical Stone Town to spice farms, pristine beaches, and hidden gems most tourists never see.

What I Do ?

Service
Airport Transfers

Comfortable and reliable pick-up from Zanzibar Airport to your hotel or destination.

Crystal Clear Kayak Trip

Paddle through the stunning turquoise waters in a transparent kayak for a unique ocean adventure.

Spice Farm Tour

Explore Zanzibar's famous spice plantations and learn about the island's rich agricultural heritage.

Prison Island Tour

Visit the historic island, meet giant tortoises, and relax on beautiful beaches.

More Services
  • Stone Town Tour: Discover the UNESCO World Heritage site with a guided walk through history.
  • Dolphin Watching: Enjoy a boat trip to see dolphins in their natural habitat.
  • Jozani Forest Tour: Explore Zanzibar’s only national park and see the rare Red Colobus monkeys.
  • Safari Blue: Experience a full-day sea adventure with snorkeling, seafood BBQ, and sandbank relaxation.
  • Mnemba Island Snorkeling: Snorkel in crystal-clear waters around the famous Mnemba Atoll.
  • City & Cultural Tours: Learn about local culture, markets, and daily life in Zanzibar.
